Hong Kong Style Macaroni Soup

Macaroni noodles in warm chicken broth with ham and vegetables. The perfect anytime meal that is a signature breakfast favorite in Hong Kong.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Ham
  • Chicken Broth
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Black Pepper
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni based on the package until al dente
  2. Drain the macaroni
  3. Cut ham into small cubes
  4. In a pot, add chicken broth and bring to a boil
  5. Add the mixed vegetables, cubed ham, macaroni and lower to a simmer
  6. Add black pepper and check seasoning
  7. Remove from heat and serve into a bowl
