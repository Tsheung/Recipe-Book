Udon

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Dashi Broth
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Green Onion

Instructions

  1. Cook udon noodles in a pot of water until firm and chewy
  2. Drain noodles from water and into a bowl
  3. Bring dashi broth to a boil
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cut green onions into broth
  5. Lower to a simmer
  6. Remove from heat and serve together into bowl
