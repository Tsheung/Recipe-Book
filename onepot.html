One Pot Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Vegetables
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Cut cherry tomatoes and vegetables into small sizes
  2. Add the cherry tomatoes, vegetables, uncooked spaghetti and tomato sauce into a pan
  3. Add 1.5 cups of water into pot
  4. Season with salt and black pepper
  5. Boil and repeatively stir until spaghetti is al dente
  6. Remove from heat and serve into a plate
